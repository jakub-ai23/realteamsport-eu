What FRESH Is

Four days on the beach. International field. Serious coaching.

FRESH is not a holiday. It's a training camp that happens to be in one of the best places in Europe. March in Mallorca means warm weather, beach courts, and a field of players from across the world who are there to get better.

Every edition sells out. Every edition ends with the same thing: people making plans to come back.

The Programme

What a FRESH week looks like

🏋️

Coaching Clinics

Morning sessions with experienced coaches. Technique, tactics, game IQ. Every level welcome — the coaching adapts.

🏆

Tournament

Competitive bracket play. Seeded by skill level. The tournament is real — so are the bragging rights.

Team dinners, evening socials, the kind of week where you show up not knowing anyone and leave with a WhatsApp group you'll actually use.
