Who We Are

Third chapter of a family company

REAL TEAM started as a family travel agency in Slovakia in 1994 — my parents built it from scratch, running school ski trips and sport travel across Central Europe for 20 years.

I took it over in 2023. Third chapter.

I've lived in Munich, worked in tourism across Europe, visited 50+ countries, played floorball for the Slovak national team, and spent too many hours organizing trips that turned into something bigger than a trip.

The CK travel agency license is still active. The spirit of "get people moving together" never changed. The type of travel did.

The Story Behind It

Not every attempt worked.

I've organized events that sold out and events that didn't. Tried building a coaching app — didn't go anywhere. Ran a podcast for two years. Moved countries twice. Started a sport from scratch in a city where nobody had heard of it.

The common thread: I keep doing it. Not because it always works, but because organizing things for people who show up is worth the effort.
